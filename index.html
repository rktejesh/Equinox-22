    Equinox Events

    • HackoFiesta

      See more

      Equinox presents Hack-o-Fiesta, Hackathon designed to inspire and empower young coders to pursue and develop their programming skills. Over the course of 36 hours, teams of 1 - 5 participants will work to develop a software project that encourages team spirit & helps to solve real-world problems.

    • Dream in Code

      See more

      Dream-in-Code, a global coding competition, where programmers of all levels put their skills to the test. Competitors work their way through a series of online algorithmic puzzles to earn a spot at the finals, all for a chance to win the championship title and take a grand prize home.It is based on ACM style rounds that continue for 3 hours unless the information about the round states other duration time.

    • Incognito V3.0

      See more

      "Computers are good at following instructions, but not at keeping your secrets." The flagship CTF event, an international contest that gathers more than 300 teams from all around the globe. Your skills will be pushed to the limits by advanced real life cyber-attack scenarios prepared by Equinox cybersecurity experts. So get ready to capture the flags and rise to the top.

    • Web Maniac

      See more

      It's time to brush up and furnish your web development skills where the participants will be given a problem statement which will be devised in such a way that will test the two subdomains of Web Development: Frontend and Backend. This challenge will test your development instincts and will surely carve out a better developer in you.

    • Appophillia

      See more

      For some, the thought of creating a mobile app from scratch sounds like an uphill task full of corny, complex coding activities. But it doesn’t have to be that way! Turn on the developer mode in your phones, assemble a team of 1 or 2 developers, pick your target problem from a set of austere but entrancing themes and unleash your creativity to create mobile app solutions using Java, Kotlin or Flutter framework.

    • Coduo

      See more

      Here, you have to participate with either your coding partner or alone to solve some algorithmic problems full of logic building and other maths mastering puzzles. Coduo will take place in two steps, one is prelims to be held on “Codechef”, and second one is finals on a platform known as “Hackerearth”. This competition is focused on testing your problem solving, logical thinking and coding skills.

  • IIIT Lucknow

    The Annual Technical Fest

    EQUINOX 22
    Equinox, the annual technical fest organized by IIIT Lucknow is back with its third edition. With the theme of this year's Equinox'22 being "Embracing the Nostalgia", we aim to take you back to the times as good as ever, with a new version of you, the one that will triumph over all the hardships and help you find a new epitome of hope and growth.

    Celebrating the coder, the reader, the learner, and the leader, Equinox provides them a platform to showcase their ideas and build upon them, to morph their unrelenting imagination into a reality. This time all your joy, excitement, and thriller will be quadrupled as we are back with many more fun exciting contests for all students, experts, and beginners to code, learn and experience the wonders of technology.

    Equinox will take you on a 3-day journey filled with adventurous and educational events that will drive you to think outside the box and leave you with a hue of reminiscent memories. To participate in fest events you must be 18 years of age. For any queries, you can contact a team member.
